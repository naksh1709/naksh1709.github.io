You can win

About author

Shiv Khera is an Indian author, activist and motivational speaker, best known for his book, You Can Win. He launched a movement against caste-based reservation in India, founded an organization called Country First Foundation.

Summary

"You Can Win" is a self-help book by Shiv Khera, first published in 1998. The book is focused on providing practical and motivational advice to help readers achieve success in their personal and professional lives. One of the strengths of "You Can Win" is its emphasis on practical application.
